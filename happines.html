Happiness in the Family

This heart is filled with anxiety, Anxieties have increased too much. There is no completely happy person in the world , apart from a baby lying in a cradle

Happy is the person who is able to flow into Nothingness without seeing the suffering of his time . Happy is the farmer, who did not reap what he sowed by mistake, and Shudgorin started anew.

I look at the brave warrior, sad , with a badge on his chest and a chorus in the streets. The past does not give him comfort, and there is no hope for the future.

Maybe the past life is all air, maybe everything you see is a mirage. Even if the place of worship for seventy-four years is Manfur Manzil, it is disgusting and ruined.

Family is a sacred place
